One quick step: add your free Cesium token

  1. Sign up free at cesium.com/ion/signup (no credit card).
  2. Open the Access Tokens tab and copy your default token (starts with eyJ…).
  3. Paste it into map.html where it says CESIUM_ION_TOKEN, then reload.

The free tier is plenty for this map — satellite imagery, world terrain, and OSM Buildings.
